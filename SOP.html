What is a Standard Operational Procedure?

A Standard Operational Procedure (SOP) is a set of step-by-step instructions compiled by an organization to help workers carry out routine operations. SOPs aim to achieve efficiency, quality output, and uniformity of performance, while reducing miscommunication and failure to comply with industry regulations.

  • Consistency: Ensures tasks are performed consistently every time.
  • Safety: Minimizes the risk of accidents and errors.
  • Training: Serves as a guide for new employees.
  • Quality Control: Helps maintain product or service quality.
