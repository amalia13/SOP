What is a Standard Operational Procedure (SOP)?

A Standard Operational Procedure (SOP) is a written document that provides step-by-step instructions to help workers carry out routine operations. SOPs are designed to ensure consistency, efficiency, and quality in organizational processes.

Why Use Standard Operational Procedures?

  • Consistency: Ensures tasks are performed the same way every time.
  • Quality: Maintains high standards and reduces errors.
  • Training: Helps new employees learn processes faster.
  • Compliance: Assists with meeting regulations and standards.
  • Efficiency: Saves time and resources by streamlining work.

Key Parts of an SOP:

  • Purpose: Why the SOP exists.
  • Scope: Where, when, and to whom the SOP applies.
  • Procedure: Step-by-step instructions.
  • Responsibilities: Who is responsible for each step.
  • Review & Update: Regular improvements and checks.
